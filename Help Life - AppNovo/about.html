Why was it created?

Help Life application was created to provide the necessary support to victims of natural disasters in every corner of the world, where all users will have access to this operating system very simply.

The Technologies

Speech To Text

will convert the voice to text. For example in a case where the film of the user's mobile device is dirty or broken. Through speech, where the victim herself is in a situation of extreme attention where she is and at the same time needing to protect herself from the phenomena that occur, she will click a button, which will ask for help by voice. This voice will be converted to text and later sent to a calendar contact immediately

Weather Company Data

which allows the sending of alerts about the weather and gives access, according to user data, possible diagnoses of diseases, allergies and so on

Gamification

By registering, providing personal data and health history, the user will accumulate points by gradually filling the application. The more updated, the more points can be converted to medicine, discounts on health plans and pharmacies agreed with the application.

Facial recognition

the application will perform facial recognition, making the victim's most detailed and unambiguous identification, proceeding to the most appropriate rescue, then directing her to the nearest available hospital.
